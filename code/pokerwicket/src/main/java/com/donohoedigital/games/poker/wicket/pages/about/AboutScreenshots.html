DD Poker main menu lets you directly access:

  • Practice
  • Online
  • Analysis
  • Poker Clock
  • Calculator Tool
  • Support and Help

DD Poker automatically tracks your tournament wins, placements and cash prizes.

Create profiles for multiple players.

Configure player profile to join or list games on the DD Poker public games list.
DD Poker comes with 15 predefined tournaments including Card Room, Online, Vegas Limit/No Limit Combo and recent World Championships.

If you don't find what you need in the pre-defined tournaments, create your own tournaments.

Define everything you need: levels, antes, blinds, buy-ins, rebuys, add-ons and payouts.

Tournament formats can be used for practice and for hosting your own tournament.
Deal high card to see who gets the button at the start of the tournament.

Sample hand - full house, with folded and mucked hands displayed. The intuitive user interface and DD Dashboard display lots of information:

  • Current rank
  • Number of tables and players left
  • Current antes and blinds and time left in level
  • Next antes and blinds
  • Pot odds, hand strength and access to the Calculator Tool
Chip races are automatically handled.
View betting history at any time during the hand.
See where you stand against the chip leaders at any time.
Get detailed, easy to understand advice from the DD Poker Advisor.
Every hand you ever play in DD Poker is saved for you to come back and analyze later. Find out which hands you play well and which ... not so well.
Host your own tournament at home. The computer counts down the time, tracks the blinds and lets you know when levels end and when rebuys/add-ons should occur.
Extensive in-game help details everything about the game and provides helpful information like poker etiquette and tips on hosting your own tournament.
Use Online Lobby to coordinate and chat with other DD Poker users.
Use the Calculator Tool's showdown calculator to determine the all-in-showdown odds like you see on the World Poker Tour or ESPN.
Use the Calculator Tool's Flop/Turn/River tabs to see how your hand can improve.
Use the Calculator Tool's Hand Ladder tab to see how your hand ranks against all possible opponent's hands.
Use the Calculator Tool's Hand Group Simulations tab to see how your hand fairs against groups of hands like Big Pairs and Suited Connectors. Customize the groups you run against too!
