Once upon a time, DD Poker was a full time business for founder Doug Donohoe and friend Greg King. Things were promising for a while until our primary competition became free play-money poker rooms funded by their real-money counterparts.

As sales slowed, Doug and Greg took full time jobs and DD Poker transformed into a side-business. The only way to compete with free is free, so DD Poker 3 is now free. However, we still incur expenses for:

  • Hosting our servers (website and the online games portal)
  • Bandwidth (downloads and the online games portal)
  • Development hardware
  • Development software (e.g., IntelliJ, Install4j, JProfiler, Photoshop, etc.)
  • Our time

If you enjoy DD Poker and wish to keep its community thriving, please consider donating to offset these expenses. If you purchased DD Poker 2 recently, please think of it as a donation.
